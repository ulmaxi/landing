Build Your own Data

The foundation of advanced analytics and machine is raw data. With Ulmax we make sure you collect and build your own valuable raw data. You have full control, ownership and access to this data. No sampling and analysis based on all your data.

Transparency

You have access to your own data and you can build and use your own machine learning algorithms and advanced analysis. This way you have full control of the whole process. No dependency on a "black box" of third parties.

Advanced Analytics

You will need a good data model to make advanced analytics and machine learning possible. Ulmax has an extensive proven data model, which allows you to start right away. This data model is flexible and you can extend it in any way you want, so it perfectly fits your situation.

Machine Learning

You want to make use of the outcomes of advanced analysis and machine learning. You can install Ulmax on your own cloud environment, so you can esily use APIs to make your data accessible. This way you can quickly and flexibly optimize and personalize your website, app and marketing channels.

Competitive Advantage

By building your own data and knowledge you also build a competitive advantage. You won't be dependent on third parties who will keep your data after terminating a contract. Besides, they can't use your data to improve their solution and your competitor's business.

Data Quality & privacy

Data is only valuable if it is of good quality and it complies with privacy regulations. We value this greately and have several modules for you to guarantee data quality and comply with privacy regulations.
